What We Do

We help software development teams struggling with various aspects of software engineering processes.

Hire us to optimize your overall operations by creating repeatable and adaptable processes which will allow your team to focus on delivering product value effectively.

We emphasize stability, predictability and continuous improvement which will lead to building a successful and thriving organization.

Our Services

Among other services, we can provide the following:

SDLC Efficiency

The primary goal of a software development team is to deliver code. Are you getting the business results you need, on the timelines you need them? We analyze your SDLC from requirements gathering to deployment to ensure you’re delivering as planned and driving the business forward.

Incident Management

When your systems go down, you need effective, proven processes for incident response and escalation; runbooks to minimize risk; and, after it all, root cause analyses (RCAs) to build the understanding and policies to prevent downtime and build quality. We help you build these from start to finish.

Recruiting, Hiring and Onboarding

We’ll develop effective hiring practices so your new hires will be setup for success and provide value from day 1 and beyond! This includes on-shore, off-shore, employees and contractors.

Team Management

Getting the most out of your software development teams and partners can be challenging, when you’re not in the business of software development. We manage your vendor/contractor relationships, as well as onshore and offshore team members; and drive the employee development strategies that create stable, effective teams.

Security Controls

Do you have any security gaps in your SDLC? We have extensive experience in SDLC risk mitigation and will make sure your code is developed safely while not sacrificing efficiency.

Finance Management

Do your projects meet their budgets – and are those budgets realistic up front? Are you properly recognizing development work as capitalizable assets? We ensure that you follow finance best practices and that your balance sheet reflects the business value delivered.
